WHAT IS HRepport?

This app is meant to help different employees in their daily job,
everyone can someday be in a situation where they feel misused,
harassed, lonely, stress or just out of place.
And this app will help you through it.
Here you will have the option to send an mail to your company
about your situation or call someone if you need to talk.
And the best thing is that you have an option to be anonyms.

In these pictures you see the sign in,
where you tap in your company’s name, username and your password.

In this page you will get an option to send in a complain or a message about what has happened,
it is very important that you explain in detail.
How it works is that, when you send in a complain,
the mail will go directly to the hr department of your company.
And they will try to solve the situation.

You can also use the talk to someone button to call a person if you are in need to talk.
If you notice that the problem isn’t solved, and you still feel harassed.
Then you can press the button called unsolved complaint and call Hrepport,
there we will contact your company and say that this problem has not been handled.

In this last picture you will see that the complaint or
rapport where sendt and no problem happened.
There is also a feedback button,
where we will be happy to hear your thoughts about the app
and if there are anything to improv.
